Prospective Students and Current Openings

  • Looking for students who want to apply for PhD program, Research Assistant, or Research Associate related positions.

Recruitment/admission requirements

  1. Bachelor's or Master's degree in information, communication, computer science or related fields;
  2. At least 79 points in TOEFL, or above 6.5 points in IELTS (with writing 6.0), or above 430 points in CET6 (College English Test band 6);
  3. Doctoral candidates should be enthusiastic about scientific research, inquisitive and well-behaved.
  4. Postdoctoral candidates with research experience or related publications in digital twinning, edge computing, and machine learning are preferred.

Scholarship/Salary

  • Doctoral students are eligible for a monthly scholarship of hk $18,000, which is sufficient to support their study and living expenses in Hong Kong.
  • Post-doctoral salary: HK $26,000-38,000, depending on the applicant's qualifications.

How to apply

  • Please send your resume, scientific research achievements/experience, published papers and other related information to email: yfu@hkmu.edu.hk; Please follow the format of 'Postdoc Application Name' or 'PhD Application Name' for the subject of the email.
